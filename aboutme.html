Hello! I'm Jane Wang and I run this page!

I started baking for as long as I can remember, but I found my passion for making French Macarons during my sophomore year of high school. As someone who didn't have a job and had no source of income, I remeber walking into a French bakery and thinking "wow those macarons are so expensive"... As a result, my sister and mom urged me to try making some. I was quite nervous to try and make these desserts at first because all my friends who attempted making them before failed horrendously, but surprinsly enough I actually succeeded on my first try. I took this as a sign to start making more uniqe flavors such as ube and rose and I ended up starting my own French Macaron Blog on Instagram where I also sold them to my high school peers. You may be wondering how I came up with the name "Westivew Macarons" but Westview was actually the name of my high school. :)
