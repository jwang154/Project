Jane Wang's Project Proposal

What is your website about?

My website will be about my baking journey and the start of my French Macaron shop. In honor of my high shool and where I first got the inspiration to make French Macarons, I plan to name my website Westview Macarons.

What is the objective of your website

I want to make this website to showcase and inspire others to try to make different flavor French Macarons and a place to document my baking joruney.

Who is your target audience

My target audience is anyone who is interested in purchasing cheap and high quality French Macarons or anyone who also has a passion for baking to use my pictures as inspiration for their own baking joruny.
